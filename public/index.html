🎮 About the Game

SportsZone Battle is a high-energy board game that blends physical mini-games 🏈🏀🏐🥎, trivia, and strategy. Compete to conquer zones, earn points, and outplay your friends using real mini-games and fast-paced action. Perfect for parties, game nights, and sports fans of all ages!

📋 How to Play

  1. 🎲 Place the board in the center and choose your player tokens.
  2. 🃏 Shuffle Sport Cards & place Challenge Decks near each zone.
  3. 🏐 Place a Mini-Ball Token in the center of each zone.
  4. 🧮 Set up the Scoreboard and grab the dice.
  5. On Your Turn:
    • 🎲 Roll both dice and move clockwise.
    • 🏃‍♂️ Enter the sport zone you land on.
    • 📤 Draw a Sport Card — it might start a mini-game, challenge, or special move!
    • ⭐ Earn points for success!
  6. Zone Control: Hold a zone for 2 full turns 🏅 = +10 points!
  7. Mini-Games & Challenges:
    • Mini-games: 🏹 Flick, toss, bounce!
    • Challenges: 🤔 Trivia, 💃 Movement puzzles, ⚔️ 1v1 matchups.
    • Win mini-game = 3 pts 🥉 | Win challenge = 2 pts 🥈
  8. Special Cards: 🎁 Bonus moves, steal points, trigger chaos!
  9. Winning: 🎉 Game ends after 8 rounds or 3 zones captured. Highest score wins!

🧩 Game Pieces

  • 📍 Game Board: 4 zones: 🏈 🥎 🏀 🏐
  • 🎯 Player Tokens: Represent where players are on the board.
  • 🏐 Mini-Ball Tokens: Used in fun mini-games!
  • 🃏 Sport Cards: Drive the action each turn.
  • 📑 Challenge Decks: Mini-game instructions, trivia, and more.
  • 📊 Scoreboard: Track the score ➡️ Player 1: 0 | Player 2: 0
  • 🎲 Dice (6 & 8-sided): Decide your movement.
  • ⏱️ Timer: 30–60 second countdowns for mini-games.
  • 💠 Tokens: Represent earned points.
  • 🏀 Mini Game Props: Hoop, launcher, cups, targets.
